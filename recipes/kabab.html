KABAB

Description

kebab, also spelled kebob or kabob, dish of Middle Eastern or Central Asian origin that typically combines small pieces of meat such as lamb or beef with vegetables on a skewer and is then grilled

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Kabab Powder
  • Oil
  • Lemon

STEPS

  1. In a mixing bowl break the eggs into a container and whisk them using a spoon.
  2. Addsalt, chili powder, kabab powder, ginger garlic paste and all the above ingredients except the chicken and mix well.
  3. Make a thick batter of flowing consistency by adding enough water little by little
  4. f the paste is too thick then you can add more lemon juice or a little water.
  5. Once a thick paste is formed, taste the marinade and then based on your taste needs you can add more of the ingredients.
